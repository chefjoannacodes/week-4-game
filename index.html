We're Off to See the Wizard!

Help Dorothy and Friends get to the Oz

You will be given a target number of steps to take on the yellow brick road at the start of the game.

There are four of Dorothy's friends below. By clicking on a friend you will add a specific amount of steps to your total steps.

You win the game by matching your total steps to the target number, and you lose the game if your total steps goes above the target number.

The value of each of Dorothy's friends is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, all her friends have new values.
